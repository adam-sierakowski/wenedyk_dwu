The French village
Long ago, in a quiet village in France, there lived a miller. That miller had three sons and two daughters. 
The oldest son wanted to become a knight one day, but his father had no money to buy a horse. 
The middle son wanted to become a monk, but the nearest monastery had no room for him. T
he youngest son didn't know what he wanted to do. We don't know what the daughters wanted, 
because stories in those days didn't talk about such things.
